Who we are

The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) is a prestigious international organization dedicated to the progress and innovation of the global automotive industry. Founded in 1905, SAE was established with the core mission of uniting scientific and technical professionals to engage in open academic discourse, drive advancements in automotive science and engineering, and accelerate the modernization of transportation technologies worldwide.
